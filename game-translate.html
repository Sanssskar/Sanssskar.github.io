📖 How to Translate Nepali to English — Step by Step

  1. Set Translate From to Nepali (or use Auto Detect).
  2. Set Translate To to English.
  3. Type or paste your Nepali text in the Source Text box.
  4. Click Translate or press Ctrl + Enter.
  5. Your English translation appears instantly in the right panel.
  6. Click Copy to save or Listen to hear pronunciation.
