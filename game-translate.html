About the Free Language Translator

This is a free online language translator that lets you instantly convert text between 20+ languages. No login, no downloads — just type and translate!

🌍 Supported Languages

English, Nepali, Hindi, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified), Arabic, Turkish, Dutch, Polish, Swedish, Indonesian, Thai, Vietnamese, and Ukrainian.

✨ Features

  • Auto Detect: Let the translator automatically identify your source language.
  • Text-to-Speech: Listen to both source and translated text using your browser's built-in speech engine.
  • Swap Languages: Quickly swap source and target languages with one click.
  • Copy to Clipboard: Copy your translation instantly.
  • Quick Chips: Jump to a target language with one tap.
  • Up to 5000 characters per translation.

💡 How to Use

  • Select your source and target languages (or leave source on Auto Detect).
  • Type or paste your text into the Source Text box.
  • Click Translate — your translation appears instantly!
  • Use Listen to hear the text spoken aloud.
  • Use Copy to copy the result to your clipboard.

Powered by the MyMemory Translation API — a free, widely-used translation service. For highly technical or critical content, always verify with a professional translator.
