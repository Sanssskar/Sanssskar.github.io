About Our Free Online Arcade Games

Welcome to the arcade games section of Sanskar Shrestha's portfolio! Here you'll find a collection of free online browser games that are fun, engaging, and completely free to play. No downloads, no installations - just pure gaming fun right in your browser.

🎮 Why Play Our Free Games?

  • Completely Free: All games are 100% free with no hidden costs or in-app purchases.
  • No Downloads: Play directly in your web browser on any device.
  • Save High Scores: Your best scores are saved locally in your browser.
  • Improve Skills: From typing speed to memory and reflexes - each game helps develop different cognitive skills.
  • Mobile Friendly: All games are responsive and work on smartphones and tablets.

🎯 Featured Games

  • Typing Speed Test: Challenge yourself with our advanced typing test. Track your WPM (Words Per Minute) and accuracy. Perfect for improving keyboard skills.
  • Classic Snake Game: The nostalgic arcade classic. Control the snake, eat food, and try to achieve the highest score without crashing.
  • Memory Match: A brain-training card game that tests and improves your short-term memory and concentration.
  • Whack-a-Mole: A fast-paced reflex game that tests how quickly you can react to moving targets.
  • Block the Pig: A strategic puzzle game that requires careful planning and logical thinking.
  • Piggy On The Run: An exciting platform runner with double jumps, dashes, and challenging obstacles.

💡 Benefits of Playing Browser Games

Playing online games isn't just about entertainment. Studies show that puzzle and strategy games can improve cognitive function, memory, and problem-solving skills. Typing games help improve keyboard proficiency and typing speed - a valuable skill for students and professionals alike. Reflex games enhance hand-eye coordination and reaction times.

🔧 Technical Details

All games are built using HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript - no external dependencies or plugins required. They work on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Games save your progress and high scores using localStorage, so your achievements persist between sessions.
